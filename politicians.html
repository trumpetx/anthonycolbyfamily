Colby Politicians

Colby, Bainbridge – of St. Louis, MO. Served in the New York State Assembly from 1901 to 1902, and was a special assistant to the United States Attorney General. Secretary of State under Woodrow Wilson from March 23, 1920 until March 4 , 1921
Colby, E. F. — of Cook County, Ill. Delegate to Illinois state constitutional convention Cook County, 1847.
Colby, Eli son of John Colby and Sarah Flanders was born 5 February 1815 in Warner, Merrimack, New Hampshire. Mayor of Little Rock, Ark., 1843-44. He died 7 May 1844 in Little Rock, Pulaski, Arkansas
Colby, H. G. O. — of New Bedford, Bristol County, Mass. Whig. Delegate to Whig National Convention from Massachusetts, 1839.
Colby, James T. — of Litchfield, Hillsborough County, N.H. Republican. Delegate to Republican National Convention from New Hampshire, 1952.
Colby, John P. — of Warner, Merrimack County, N.H. Member of New Hampshire state house of representatives from Warner, 1863-64.
Colby, Kenneth P. — of Keene, Cheshire County, N.H. Republican. Alternate delegate to Republican National Convention from New Hampshire, 1952.
Colby, Kimball G. son of Joseph Kimball Colby and Mary Ada Gleason was born 9 February 1873 in Methuen, Essex, Massachusetts. Republican. Delegate to Republican National Convention from Massachusetts, 1924.
Colby, Michael — of New Hampshire. Democrat. Delegate to Democratic National Convention from New Hampshire, 2000.
Colby, Nathan S. — of Warner, Merrimack County, N.H. Member of New Hampshire state house of representatives from Warner, 1829-30, 1833-34.
Colby, Philip, Jr. — of Warner, Merrimack County, N.H. Member of New Hampshire state house of representatives from Warner, 1835-36.
Colby, Samuel W. — of Warner, Merrimack County, N.H. Member of New Hampshire state house of representatives from Warner, 1857-58.
Colby, Simon P. — of Weare, Hillsborough County, N.H. Member of New Hampshire state senate 3rd District, 1842-44.
Colby, Thomas W. — of Hopkinton, Merrimack County, N.H. Member of New Hampshire state senate 8th District, 1823-24.
Colby, Wesley J. son of John H. & Ruth Colby — of Laconia, Belknap County, N.H. Republican. Mayor of Laconia, N.H., 1988-90.
